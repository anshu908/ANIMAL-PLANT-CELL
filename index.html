Understanding Animal and Plant Cells

Animal Cell

The animal cell is a eukaryotic cell that contains membrane-bound organelles. Key components include the nucleus, mitochondria, endoplasmic reticulum, Golgi apparatus, and lysosomes. Animal cells lack a cell wall but have a flexible plasma membrane.

Plant Cell

The plant cell is also a eukaryotic cell but differs from animal cells by having a rigid cell wall, chloroplasts, and a large central vacuole. These features support photosynthesis and provide structural support.
